Our Documentaries

The Rise of the Gig Economy

explores how digital platforms and technology have transformed traditional jobs into flexible, short-term gigs. It highlights opportunities for workers’ autonomy, the challenges of job insecurity, and the evolving labor market landscape globally and in India.

Featuring:
1. Prof. Tejbir Singh Rana 2. CA Parag Gupta
3. Mr. Sandeep Jangra 4. Mr. Charchit Kurra

"Saffron, White and Green: A century of Indian Independence"

reflects on India's journey over the last hundred years, highlighting its struggles, achievements, and transformations. It inspires appreciation for the nation's resilience, unity, and cultural heritage, celebrating the spirit of freedom that defines India.

Featuring:
1. Ms. Srishti Sharma 2. Ms. Meghna Joshi
3. Prof. Nitin Puri 4. Dr. Virender Bhardwaj

Urja: A Mega What on India's Future Energy

explores India's current energy sector challenges and investigates sustainable solutions, focusing on renewable energy strategies to power the nation’s future, highlighting both environmental and economic impacts in the transition to clean sources.

Featuring:
1. Ajinkya Shinde 2. Shilpy Gupta

Error 302 : Request for Cyber Privacy

explores how cyber threats exploit system vulnerabilities, highlighting critical risks associated with 302 errors and the urgent need for robust cybersecurity practices to protect data, networks, and digital infrastructure.

Featuring:
1. Ms. Sailaja Vadlamudi 2. Dr. Sunaina Kanojia 3. Mr. Akash Kamal Mishra
4. Prof. Sandeep Shukla 5. Dr. Omkar Rai 6. Mr. Urvin Mistry

Climate is Changing, Are You?

explores how global warming and extreme weather affect our world, challenging viewers to evaluate their daily choices and adopt more sustainable practices to help combat climate change.

Featuring:
1. Dr. C.K. Varshney 2. Shri Harish Dhawan 3. Pinaki Dasgupta
4. Prof. Usha Tandon 5. Dr. Navindu Gupta 6. Dr. Usha
7. Dr. Iyatta M. Uprety 8. Neha Sinha 9. Ankan De
