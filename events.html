Flagship Event

Ace of Spade

Our annual flagship event, Ace of Spade, is the highlight of the academic year. It's a grand celebration of economics, strategy, and talent, attracting over 400 students for a series of thrilling competitions and insightful speaker sessions.

  • Ace the Case: A national-level case study competition.
  • Inflation Auction: A dynamic bidding challenge.
  • Brand Wars: A creative battle of marketing prowess.
